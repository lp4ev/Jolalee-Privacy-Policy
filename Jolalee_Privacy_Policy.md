# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 17 November 2025

Thank you for using our Telugu Rhymes App for Kids ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### **1.1. Personal Information**

We **do not collect**, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Location
* Photos / media from your device

### **1.2. Non‑Personal Information**

The App may automatically collect basic, non‑identifiable information to improve performance and user experience. This may include:

* Device type (Android version, device model)
* App usage analytics (e.g., screens visited, time spent)
* Crash logs

This information is **anonymous** and cannot identify you.

---

## 2. Permissions Used

The App may request certain Android permissions solely for delivering core functionality.

### **2.1. Internet Access**

Used only to:

* Load thumbnails or images
* Stream or download rhyme audio/video (if applicable)
* Show safe, child‑appropriate content

### **2.2. Storage Access (If Used)**

* To cache rhymes or thumbnails for faster playback.
* No files are accessed outside the app's sandbox.

We do **not** access any personal files, images, or documents on the user’s device.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

Non‑personal information may be used to:

* Improve app performance and stability
* Diagnose issues and crashes
* Enhance user experience

No personal data is collected, shared, or sold.

---

## 4. Data Sharing & Third‑Party Services

We do **not** share your personal information with any third parties.

If the app uses third‑party SDKs (e.g., Google Firebase Analytics, AdMob), they may collect anonymous analytics data as per their own privacy policies.

All such data is:

* Aggregated
* Non‑identifiable
* Used solely for improving app performance

We do not enable personalized ads for children.

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data from:

* Unauthorized access
* Misuse
* Loss

Since the App does not store personal data, risk is minimal.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
